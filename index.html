About me

Electrical engineer, studied in Uruguay and France, currently doing an internship at Deezer as part of the "ATIAM: Acoustics, signal processing, and computer science applied to music" masters from IRCAM/Sorbonne University (Paris).

Studying in Paris with the support of a scholarship from ANII, Uruguay's national research and innovation agency.

My interests are in signal processing, machine learning and music information retrieval, as well as research openness, tech accessibility and ethics in the design and use of machine learning models.

Amateur violinist who loves tango, murga, and folk.
